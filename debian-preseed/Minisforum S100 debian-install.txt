Guide for booting Minisforum S100 with linux:

Prepare a USB stick with a writeable part (Rufus)
Grab the correct modules:

./usr/lib/modules/6.12.48+deb13-amd64/kernel/fs/ufs/ufs.ko.xz
./usr/lib/modules/6.12.48+deb13-amd64/kernel/drivers/ufs/core/ufshcd-core.ko.xz
./usr/lib/modules/6.12.48+deb13-amd64/kernel/drivers/ufs/host/ufshcd-pci.ko.xz
./usr/lib/modules/6.12.43+deb13-amd64/kernel/fs/ufs/ufs.ko.xz

by copying them to the installation media

Do not choose automated install, but rather "install"
When the partitioner shows you only the USB, ESC out and choose shell

There, go to the modules and modprobe them into the installation (using -f to force)
by specifying:

modprobe -f ./<module-name>

With exit return to the installer and continue with 'detect disks'
